Pulled Pork Barbecue

  • Time: 12 Hours 25 Minutes
  • Yield: 16 servings
Cake:
Ingredients Quantity
All-Purpose Flour 1 cup
Baking Powder 1 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Eggs 5, separated
Sugar 1 cup
Milk 1/3 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Tres Leches Mixture:
Ingredients Quantity
Heavy Cream 1/4 cup
Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 14-ounce can
Evaporated Milk 1 12-ounce can
Icing:
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 5
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1.5 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9 by 13 inch cake pan.

Step 2: Sift the flour, baking powder and a salt into a bowl.

Step 3: Make sure the eggs are separated. Beat the egg yolks with 3/4 cups of sugar on high speed until the yolks are pale yellow color. Stir in the milk and vanilla. Pour the egg yolk mixture over the flour mixture and stir slowly until combined.

Step 4: Now, beat the egg whites on high speed until it has a soft mixture. With the mixer still on, pour in the rest of the 1/4 cups of sugar and beat the eggs until they are stiff -but not dry-. Fold the egg white mixture slowly and gently until combined. Scoop into the prepared pan and spread to even out the surface.

Step 5 Bake the cake for 35 to 40 minutes or until it is cooked thoroughly. Allow to cool in the pan, and then turn upside down onto a platter.

Step 6: Combine the heavy cream, evaporated milk and condensed milk in a small pitcher. Pierce the surface of the cake with a fork several times. Drizzle the milk mixture over the top and allow the mixture to be absorbed into the cake.

Step 7: Whip the cream with the sugar. Spread it evenly over the top and sides of the cake.

Step 8: Refrigerate until it is ready to be served.
